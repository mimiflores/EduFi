What Is Personal Finance?

Personal finance is comprised of different elements pertaining to investing, budgeting, saving, and debt.

Investing

Investing is a way to potentially increase your money over time by generating income.

Budgeting

Budgeting is achieved by estimating your income and expenses over a certain period of time.

Saving

After all obligatory expenses have been paid, the surplus of funds is refered to as that individual's savings. Saving is also referred to when talking about the two types of bank accounts.

Debt

Debt is considered anything that is owed to one person by another.
